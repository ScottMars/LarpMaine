Memeotica is a Web3 strategy card game where players vote on real-world political events, generate meme tokens, earn collectible trading cards, and compete in future PvP battles. It's built on the Solana blockchain and evolves in real time with global politics.

A new political vote is held every 4 hours. Players can vote on real-world events (e.g., elections, policy outcomes). The winning side triggers the creation of a themed meme token.

Anyone who buys $50+ worth of a vote's winning meme token receives a special airdrop. If you hold 1M+ tokens, you unlock a lootbox with 5 themed trading cards.

Cards represent politicians, scandals, policies, events, and factions. They're collectible in the early phases, but once battles launch, they'll be used to build decks and fight in PvP matches for additional rewards.

PvP battle mechanics are part of the Q4 roadmap. Players will be able to use their cards to compete in ranked matches and faction wars.

The Political Season Pass is a progression system where players earn XP by voting, collecting, and battling. A free track is available to all users, while the premium track (launching later) includes exclusive rewards and cosmetics.

The Meme Archive is your personal collection of all Memeotica cards. You can view your decks, prestige upgraded cards, and badge-tagged achievements based on real-world participation.
